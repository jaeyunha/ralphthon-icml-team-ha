# AC Meta-Review Phase

Entry requires terminal discussion facts and all four final reviewer justifications. Publish the ten required sections: main contribution, agreed strengths, decisive concerns, rebuttal effect, remaining issues, reviewer disagreement, validation evidence, recommendation, confidence, and constructive next steps. Cite evidence, explain expertise/confidence weighting, preserve dissent, engage the strongest opposing argument, and directly recommend `accept` or `reject`. Score averaging is a checker rejection.
