# AC Discussion Moderation Phase

Use issue-specific threads only. Open issues from score spread, contradictory facts, theorem or novelty disagreement, unresolved decisive concerns, low-quality or non-responsive reviews, likely AC opposition, or conflicting validation interpretations. Named reviewers answer independently. Summarize and close, or open one narrower follow-up. Consensus is unnecessary; score oscillation without new evidence becomes `irreducibly_disputed`. Transition only when every termination fact is true.
